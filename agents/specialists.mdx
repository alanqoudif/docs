## Sara: documents and reports

Sara is suitable for formal reports, business plans, contracts, feasibility studies, and internal memos. It usually uses `create_doc` or `update_doc`, and can produce Markdown content that can later be exported.

## Nasser: strategy and business

Nasser is suitable for market analysis, business models, pricing, go-to-market, comparisons, and growth plans. In practice, its role appears as smarter prompt shaping for a document, spreadsheet, or presentation.

## Layla: creative and marketing

Layla is suitable for campaigns, landing pages, product names, taglines, social media content, and verbal identity. It usually uses `create_html` or `create_doc`.

## Omar: operations and execution

Omar is suitable for SOPs, roadmaps, launch plans, execution schedules, and task distribution. It uses documents and spreadsheets depending on the requested output format.

## Mira: data and analysis

Mira is suitable for spreadsheets, KPIs, financial forecasts, comparisons, and numerical analysis. Its closest tool is `create_sheet`, and it can also enrich Sara and Nasser reports with tables.

## Kimi Claw: 24/7 monitoring

Kimi Claw represents the monitoring and alerts experience in the product and marketing. In the current implementation, interfaces such as `KimiClawDock` and status components exist, but you should not assume a fully independent monitoring system for every user unless a dedicated backend has been enabled.

<Warning>
When writing official content about Kimi, memory, or continuous monitoring, distinguish the marketing vision from what is currently enabled in the backend.
</Warning>
