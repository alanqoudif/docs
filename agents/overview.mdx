## How does Mibyan choose an agent?

The frontend sends the user's message to `/api/public/agent-run`. Atlas analyzes the intent, reads the conversation and Artifact context, then chooses the appropriate tool or generation path. In the code, agents are defined in `src/data/mubayyan.ts`, while execution personas appear inside `agent-run.ts`.

## Output patterns

Agents do not have fully separate output systems; they route work through the same platform tools:

- Markdown documents through `create_doc`.
- HTML pages through `create_html`.
- XLSX spreadsheets through `create_sheet`.
- JSON/deck presentations through `create_slides` or `generate-deck`.
- Multi-page websites through `create_website`.
- Edits through `update_doc` and `update_html` after reading the current Artifact.
